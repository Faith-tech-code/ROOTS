Redefining Responsible Fatherhood

We believe that for families to be strong, men must be engaged. The "absent father" is not the problem, but a symptom. Our 'Men Matter' labs are safe, powerful workshops for young men (15-30) to discuss and redefine masculinity. We tackle financial planning, co-parenting, communication, and the legal/emotional responsibilities of fatherhood. We are building a generation of men who are proud to be present, responsible partners and fathers.

Core Workshop Themes

Responsible Masculinity

Exploring positive identity and rejecting harmful stereotypes.

Family Financial Planning

Practical skills for budgeting and providing for a family.

Co-Parenting & Communication

Learning to be an effective partner and engaged father.
