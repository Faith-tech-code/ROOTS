Muli mutya? We are here for you. If you are a young mother, a girl who has dropped out of school, a young man who wants to be a responsible father, or you are struggling and need support, we are here. All your messages are confidential. We will never share your story without your permission.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs for Beneficiaries)

Is this really free?

Yes. Our Support Hub services (counseling, legal aid, daycare) and our "Smart Choices" / "Men Matter" workshops are completely free. The MomPreneur Academy is also free for those who are selected.

Am I qualified for the MomPreneur Academy?

We are looking for commitment, not experience. If you are a single mother or at-risk woman with a strong desire to build a business, we want you to apply.

Will anyone find out I came here?

No. Your privacy is our number one priority. We will not tell your family, friends, or community.

What if I don't live nearby?

Please contact us anyway. We may have partner organizations in your area we can connect you with.
