This is our economic empowerment engine. The MomPreneur Academy is an intensive training program for single mothers, unemployed graduates, and at-risk young women. We don't just teach a skill; we build a business. Participants learn entrepreneurship, business management, financial literacy, digital skills (like graphic design), and practical vocations (like tailoring and crafts).

How it Works:

  • Train: 12 weeks of intensive skills and business training.
  • Launch: Graduates receive a 'Roots Made' startup kit (e.g., a sewing machine, laptop, or initial materials).
  • Sell: We provide a path to market through our 'Roots Made' online shop and partner markets.
