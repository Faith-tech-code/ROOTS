Education for Prevention

The best time to break a cycle is before it begins. Our 'Smart Choices' curriculum is a values-based, age-appropriate program for youth aged 12-24. We partner with schools and community centers to deliver engaging workshops on sexual/reproductive health, consent, peer pressure, healthy relationships, and financial goal-setting. We are raising a generation of informed, responsible decision-makers.

What We Teach

Consent & Boundaries

Understanding and respecting personal boundaries in all relationships.

Health & Wellness

Age-appropriate sexual and reproductive health education.

Financial Literacy

Setting financial goals and learning the basics of money management.

Emotional Intelligence

Developing self-respect, empathy, and healthy communication skills.

Bring 'Smart Choices' to Your Community
