A Foundation for Success

A mother cannot build a business if her child is not safe. A woman cannot heal if she has no support. Our Support Hubs are the 'safe haven' that makes all our other work possible. These physical centers provide the practical, wrap-around support families need to thrive.

Our Wrap-Around Services

Subsidized Daycare

Safe, quality, affordable childcare so mothers can work and train.

Legal Aid Clinics

Pro-bono legal advice for child support, land rights, and business registration.

Counseling & Wellness

Confidential mental health support for trauma and stress.
